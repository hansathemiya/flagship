Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for peak season (December to March) and 1-2 months for other times. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 30 days before departure receive a full refund minus a 10% administrative fee. Cancellations between 15-30 days receive a 50% refund. No refunds are given for cancellations within 14 days of departure.

Do you offer custom tour packages?

Yes! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and travel style. Contact us with your preferences and we'll design the perfect tour for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and PayPal. A 30% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due 30 days before your tour start date.
