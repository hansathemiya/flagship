Who We Are

Founded in 2005, Flagship Tours has grown from a small family-run business to Sri Lanka's premier travel agency. Our journey began with a simple passion: to share the beauty of our homeland with the world.

Today, we're proud to have hosted over 15,000 travelers from 42 countries, each receiving our signature blend of warm Sri Lankan hospitality and professional service.

Our Journey

2005: Humble Beginnings

Founded by travel enthusiast Ravi Perera with just two employees and a passion for showcasing Sri Lanka's beauty.

2010: First Milestone

Hosted our 1000th guest and expanded operations with a new office in Colombo and a team of 10 travel experts.

2015: Awards & Recognition

Received "Best Travel Agency in Sri Lanka" award from the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.

Present Day

A team of 35 travel professionals creating unforgettable experiences for travelers from around the world.
